Party Jollof Rice

Servings: 6-8 Cook Time: 1hr Difficulty: Easy
Rice Nigerian Party Jollof One-Pot

A vibrant, one-pot Nigerian Jollof Rice bursting with flavors. Perfect for parties and gatherings, this dish is a staple in West African cuisine.

By: African Recipes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 6 fresh tomatoes, blended
  • 2 red bell peppers, blended
  • 1–2 Scotch bonnets, blended (to taste)
  • 2 onions (1 blended, 1 sliced)
  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 3–4 cups chicken or vegetable stock
  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Optional: mixed vegetables & protein of choice

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, bell peppers, Scotch bonnets, and 1 onion. Simmer 10–15 minutes to reduce.
  2. Heat oil; sauté sliced onion. Stir in tomato paste for 2–3 minutes, then add pepper blend and cook until thick and glossy.
  3. Season with curry, thyme, bay leaves, seasoning cubes, salt, and pepper.
  4. Rinse rice; stir into sauce to coat evenly.
  5. Add stock to just cover rice. Cover tightly and cook on low for 25–30 minutes.
  6. Check doneness; add a splash of stock if needed. Fold in veggies (optional) and finish on low.
